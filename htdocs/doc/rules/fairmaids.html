Fair Maids

Spider type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Group all the cards in sets of 13 cards in descending sequence by alternate color from King to Ace and move such sets to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Will o' the Wisp, but sequences are by alternate color, and four cards per pile.
