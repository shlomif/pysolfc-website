Stonewall

Raglan type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Flower Garden, but every other card in the tableau columns are dealt face-down (the first, third, and fifth cards are face-down while the second, fourth, and sixth are face-up), and the columns are built down by alternate color.
