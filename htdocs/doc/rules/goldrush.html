Gold Rush

Klondike type. 1 deck. 2 redeals.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Klondike, but with two redeals. During the first round, cards are dealt from the talon three at a time, and then during each subsequent round, one fewer card is dealt from the talon with each draw.
