Panopticon

Yukon type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Ten Across, but with eight piles of six cards each (arranged in a similar pattern, with five face-up and one face-down card on the outer piles, and two face-up cards with four face-downs in the center piles), and four cards in the reserve.
