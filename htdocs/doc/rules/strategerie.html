Strategerie

Numerica game type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Numerica, but with four free cells. Cards from the talon can be moved to the free cells instead of the tableau piles, but each free cell can only hold one card. Cards can be moved from the free cells to the tableau or foundations, but cards from the tableau cannot be moved to free cells.
