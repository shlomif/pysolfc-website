Relaxed Golf

Golf type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the waste stack.

Quick Description

Just like Golf, but sequences do wrap around, i.e. Twos and Kings may be placed on Aces and Queens and Aces may be placed on Kings.
