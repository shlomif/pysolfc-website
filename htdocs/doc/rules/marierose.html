Marie Rose

Forty Thieves type. 3 decks. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

A three deck variation of Josephine.

Like Forty Thieves, but with three decks and sixty dealt cards. Also, sequences of cards may be moved together.
