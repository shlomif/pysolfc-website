Yukon Kings

Yukon type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Arrange all cards into four columns with complete sequences from king to ace, built down by alternate color.

Quick Description

Just like Yukon, but with no foundations. To win the game, the cards must be organized into four piles, built down from king to ace by alternate color. Difficult.
