Tile Puzzle

Puzzle type. One deck. No redeals.

Object

Restore game pieces to their proper order.

Rules

The pieces are dealt to a grid, based on the size of the puzzle. You may swap any two pieces freely. Your goal is to arrange all the pieces to successfully, and accurately, complete the picture.
