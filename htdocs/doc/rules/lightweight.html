Lightweight

Beleaguered Castle type. 2 decks. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Rules

The tableau cards are dealt in twelve piles of eight card. The eight aces are dealt to start the foundations. Tableau piles are built down by rank, regardless of suit. Any valid sequence of cards can be moved between tableau piles. Only kings can be played in empty piles.

The foundations are built up by same suit. Cards on the foundations can't be moved. The game is won if all cards are moved to the foundations.
