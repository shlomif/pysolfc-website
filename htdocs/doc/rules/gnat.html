Gnat

Numerica type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Numerica, but the aces are dealt to the foundations at the start, and the foundations are built up by suit. Also, six cards are dealt to reserve piles, which can be moved to appropriate foundations at any time.
