Fifteens

Pairing game type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the single foundation.

Rules

Sixteen cards are dealt to the tableau. Pairs of cards that are both ranked 9 or lower that total fifteen can be moved to the foundation, and the spaces are filled from the talon. Larger groups of cards that total 15 can also be discarded by moving all of them to a special reserve pile.

Tens and picture cards can only be moved in a sequence of one card of each type. Similar to larger groups of 15, they are discarded by moving all four cards to the reserve.

The game is won if all cards have been discarded.

Notes

Autodrop is disabled for this game.
