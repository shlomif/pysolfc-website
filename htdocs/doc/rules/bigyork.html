Big York

Spider type. 3 decks. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

A three deck variation of York.

Like Spider, but all cards are dealt to the tableau at the start, in fourteen columns (twelve with eleven cards and two with twelve), and sequences are allowed to turn the corner from ace to king.
