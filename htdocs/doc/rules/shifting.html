Shifting

Numerica game type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Numerica, but in addition to moving cards from the talon to the tableau, you are allowed to move single cards between tableau piles, building down by rank regardless of suit, and kings can be moved to empty piles.
