Skiz

Tarock type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Rules

This is a FreeCell type of game. Cards on the tableau build down in rank by suit. Cards build up in rank on the foundations. A stack can be moved if the cards are in descending rank order regardless of the suit. Only a King or the highest trump can be played on an empty row.

Strategy

The foundations are less important early in the game than building movable stacks. Use the reserve stacks carefully.

Author
