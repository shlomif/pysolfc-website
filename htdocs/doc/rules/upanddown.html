Up and Down

Pairing game type. 2 decks. 2 redeals.

Object

Move all cards to the single foundation.

Quick Description

Like Pyramid, but with two decks and the cards are arranged into two pyramids, similar to Two Pyramids, but with one inverted similar to Triangle.
