Petal

FreeCell type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like FreeCell, but four cards of the same rank are dealt to the foundations at the start, and the foundations are built up from that rank, turning the corner from king to ace as necessary. Also, a cards is dealt to each of the four free cells at the start of the game.
