Flamenco

Gypsy type. 2 decks. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Gypsy, but all the tableau cards are dealt face-up, and an ace and king of each suit are dealt to the foundations at the start of the games. The foundations with the kings are built down by same suit from king to ace.
