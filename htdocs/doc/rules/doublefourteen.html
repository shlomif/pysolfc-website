Double Fourteen

Pairing game type. 2 decks. No redeal.

Object

Discard all pairs of cards that add up to fourteen in rank.

Quick Description

Like Fourteen, but with two decks and eighteen playing piles.

Notes

Autodrop is disabled for this game.
