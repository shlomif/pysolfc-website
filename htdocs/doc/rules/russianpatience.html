Russian Patience (Die Russische)

Klondike type. 2 stripped decks. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Rules

This game is played with two stripped decks, consisting of the seven through king and ace of each suit. One ace is dealt to the foundations, and the remaining cards are dealt evenly to seven tableau piles.

The foundations build up in suit starting with Ace, then from Seven up to King.

The row stacks build down in alternate color. Sequences may be moved, and spaces may be filled by any single card.
