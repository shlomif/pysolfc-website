Big Alhambra

Three-Deck game type. 3 decks. 2 redeals.

Object

Move all the cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Alhambra, but with three decks and ten piles. For the third deck, the black foundations are built up from ace to king while the red foundations are built down from king to ace.
