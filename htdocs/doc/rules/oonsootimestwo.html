Oonsoo

Hanafuda type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Arrange all twelve suits in order.

Rules

When the hand is dealt there are two rows of six cards face down with six more face up on top. You can play a card on another card if it's in the same suit and in descending rank order. The third and fourth rank cards are interchangeable for all suits except Rain. Plum 4 on Plum 3 is OK. Plum 3 on Plum 4 is OK. Gaji can only be played on Rain 3.

Strategy

Hint: try to keep a row open to the canvas.
