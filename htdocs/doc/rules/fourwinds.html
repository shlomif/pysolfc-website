Four Winds

One-Deck game type. 1 deck. 1 redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Rules

The aces are dealt to the foundations, with the two aces of each suit on a different side of the talon. Four cards are dealt to free cells next to each of the foundations.

Cards are dealt one at a time from the talon. Cards in the talon can be moved to free cells or foundations. After the initial cards are moved, only cards of the same suit as the adjacent foundation can be moved back to those cells. Only one redeal is allowed.

Foundations are built up by the same suit. The game is won if all cards are moved to the foundations.
