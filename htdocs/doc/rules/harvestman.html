Harvestman

Spider type. 2 decks. No redeal.

Object

Group all the cards in sets of 13 cards in descending sequence by suit from King to Ace and move such sets to the foundation.

Quick Description

Like Arachnida, but tableau piles are built down by same suit or same rank, but combined sequences that contain groups that are both the same rank or suit cannot be moved.
