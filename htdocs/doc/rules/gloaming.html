Gloaming

Numerica type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Rules

All of the cards are dealt into three reserve piles (2 with 17 cards and one with 18). Cards can be moved from these reserve piles to one of five tableau piles or the foundations.

Cards can be placed in tableau piles regardless of rank or suit, but cards in tableau piles can only be moved to foundations.

Foundations are built up by suit, starting with aces. The game is won if all cards are moved to foundations.
