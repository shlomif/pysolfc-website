Hospital Patience

One-Deck game type. 1 deck. Unlimited redeals.

Object

Move all cards to foundations.

Rules

Cards are dealt from the talon three at a time, and can be moved to foundations. Unlimited redeals are allowed. There are eight foundations, four built up by suit from ace to king, and four built down by suit from king to ace. The game is won if all cards are moved to the foundations - it doesn't matter how many cards are in each foundation.
