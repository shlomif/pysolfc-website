Relaxed Clock

One-Deck game type. 1 deck. No redeals.

Object

Move all cards to the correct pile in the tableau, before the fourth king is revealed, and the extra pile you drew a card from is filled.

Quick Description

Just like Clock, but once per game, after all four kings have been revealed, you are allowed to take a face-down card from another pile and continue playing.
