Empress of Italy

Terrace type. 4 decks. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

A four deck variation of Queen of Italy.

Like Terrace, but with four decks, 11 tableau piles, and 19 cards starting in the reserve. ALso, empty cards in the tableau are not automatically filled from the waste pile or stock. Instead, any other card from the tableau or waste can be used to fill empty piles.
