Free Ishido Relaxed

Ishido game type. 2 decks. No redeal.

Object

Move all tiles to the playing area.

Quick Description

Like Ishido, but tiles do not need to be placed adjacent to other tiles, and there are no restrictions when placing a tile next to two or more other tiles - they just have to match the color or symbol of each.
