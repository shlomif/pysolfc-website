Kiev

Spider type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Group all the cards in sets of 13 cards in descending sequence by suit from King to Ace and move such sets to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Russian Spider, but with a different layout consisting of seven piles of four cards each (with only the top card of each visible). The remaining cards are in the talon, and when there are no moves left, you can deal one to each tableau pile.
