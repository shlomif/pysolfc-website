Simon Jester

Spider type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Group all the cards in sets of 13 cards in descending sequence by suit from King to Ace and move such sets to the foundations.

Quick Description

Just like Spider, but all cards are dealt at the beginning to the 14 piles in a triangular layout. A two-deck variation of Simple Simon.
