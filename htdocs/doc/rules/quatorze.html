Quatorze

Pairing game type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Discard all the cards.

Rules

The object is to use up all the cards from the tableau by discarding pairs of cards with ranks that total 14 (Jacks are 11, Queens are 12, and Kings are 13). You can only discard pairs if the cards are in the same row or column.

After removing a pair of cards, cards are dealt to fill in the gaps. Once the stock is empty, the cards are shifted up to fill in.

You win when the cards are all discarded.

Notes

Autodrop is disabled for this game.
